## About Me

I am **Zhenye Fan**, a Ph.D. student at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT).

My research focuses on **compiler fuzzing**, **software testing**, and **AI for software engineering**.

## Research Interests

- 🔍 **Compiler Fuzzing** - Automated testing techniques for compilers
- 🐛 **Software Testing** - Bug detection and test case generation  
- 🤖 **AI for Software Engineering** - Applying machine learning to software development
